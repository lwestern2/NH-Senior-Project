Welcome to Nauvoo House

The Nauvoo House Difference

At Nauvoo House, we understand that your BYU Idaho experience starts and ends with where you live. We want your experience here to be great so we work hard to make the part we play in that great for you.

Nauvoo is a favorite for many students at BYU Idaho for a lot of reasons. At the end of the day, the Nauvoo House experience hinges on 3 simple things: Clean, Safe, and Fun living. We strive to make these 3 things our main goals. It's just that simple.

The Nauvoo Culture

Clean Each apartment is sparkling clean and ready for you to relax in with regular cleaning inspections.
Safe Safe is No. 1 and the crew here at Nauvoo strives to keep the property beautiful and safe.
Fun Nauvoo is well known for its great socials and events. We want your time here to be Fun!

Nauvoo House is social...Come be social with us!

Nauvoo House has a social culture unlike any other apartment complex at BYU Idaho. We work hard to make sure that your roommates and neighbors are also your friends. From opening socials each semester to a clubhouse, Fire Pit and other social areas Nauvoo is built around your positive social and educational experience.
