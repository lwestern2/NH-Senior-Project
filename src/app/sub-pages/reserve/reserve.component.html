Amenity Reservation

Nauvoo House amenities are free for tenants to use. We just ask that you help us keep them nice and clean for years to come by cleaning up after yourselves.

How to reserve an amenity:

      1. Look at your desired reservation date and time on the calendar below to make sure that amenity is not already reserved.
      2. If it is available, please fill out the form to reserve the amenity; putting the date, time and your contact information.
      3. Nauvoo House management will then receive your reservation request and will either approve or deny it (Reservations may be denied if the amenity is already reserved.)
      4. Nauvoo House management will send you an email with the their approval response and any other instructions you may need about the amenity.

(Please note that reservations for weekends must be submitted on later than Friday at 4:30PM. Management is not in the office over the weekend and thus will not be able to process your reservation. Please plan accordingly.)
